Georgi Spasov

Elixir pt 1

Georgi has been working at Quanterall for more than 4 years. He is experienced in designing and implementing backend architectures and has crafted a lot of Android applications while in the company. Now he is a Project Manager at Quanterall and is leading a team of mobile developers.

Education: Varna Free University with a Bachelor’s Degree in Software Engineering and has spent a semester in the Business Academy Aarhus in Denmark.

Work experience: Georgi has been part of multiple projects in Quanterall as a project manager, lead developer or just as a team member . He has been in the functional programming field for years and has gained his knowledge by working side by side with other developers.

Here are some of the Elixir projects that he has been part of:

  • Aeternity wallet - lead developer in creating the wallet API. Implementing Bitcoin proposals BIP-32 and BIP-39. Implemented encryption functionality.
  • Aeternity Blockchain - lead role in the development of their Elixir based blockchain.
  • Cash register communication platform - lead full-stack developer of the project.

What is Georgi’s professional stack?

  • Programming languages: Elixir, Erlang, Java, JavaScript
  • Database systems: PostgreSQL, DynamoDB

A lecturer and mentor at the following courses:

  • Elixir pt 1

Ivaylo Valkov

System Administrator at Quanterall

Ivaylo is a system administrator with extensive experience in server operating systems, virtualization, containerization and orchestration. He has more than 10 years of professional experience in the System Administration and DevOps fields. Beside system administration, he had worked as backend developer, supervisor, and project manager as well.

Education: Ivaylo has graduated from the Technical University of Varna with a bachelor’s degree in Industrial Automation, and has a Master’s degree in Computer Science.

Work experience: Ivaylo worked as customer support for a vendor of VOIP, video, wireless and telecommunication equipment and solutions. He also worked as a backend web developer, hardware technical support, System and Network administrator for various businesses. Ivaylo was also a supervisor of a team of software developers for QA testing а software framework project in Python for cloud installation, automation, and management.

Ivaylo was a lecturer to the students of the HighSchool of Mathematics in Varna, the enthusiasts at VarnaLab, and the IT team for the Varna Municipality. He is committed to the free and open source software community and is doing his best to promote it as much as he can. Ivaylo wrote а manual for the installation of Slackware 10 in Bulgarian. It was uploaded to the Bulgarian GNU/Linux portal linux-bg.org. Ivaylo was part of the Bulgarian GNOME translation team. He started his own project, called Linterna Magica - a tool for watching video in browsers without using Adobe Flash, when Flash was still trending.

What is Ivaylo’s professional stack?

  • OS: Debian GNU/Linux
  • Editor: GNU Emacs
  • Programming languages: Perl, Python, Shell/Bash scripting, JavaScript, C/C++ (limited)
  • Distributions with significant usage: Trisquel, Debian, Ubuntu, CentOS, Gentoo, Slackware
  • Virtualization experience: OpenStack, Qemu/KVM, Libvirt, LXC, Docker, Kubernetes, OpenVZ
  • Version control systems: svn, git, csv
  • Cloud experience: OpenStack, Google Cloud Platform, DigitalOcean, AWS (limited)
  • Daily tools: GNU Emacs, org-mode, GNU coreutils, Bash, ssh
  • Other: Make(file), Texinfo, LaTeX, Ceph, MySQL, RabbitMQ

Teaches the following courses:

  • GNU/Linux System Administration
