Quanterall is the only academy in Southeast Europe that teaches functional programming.

We are passionate about knowledge & experience sharing.

Shaping the talent of tomorrow!

We are proud of our long term partnerships with schools and universities in Bulgaria:

Varna Free University

For many years Quanterall enjoys the opportunity to maintain a long time partnership with Varna Free University.

We partner with the University by being part of their Bioinformatics program. Quanterall Smart Clothes prorotype was also build with the help of the Varna Free University.

University of Economics, Varna

We are pleased to have the unique opportunity to maintain a partnership with the talented and ambitious students from the University of Economics, Varna. In a collaborative format, we will exchange ideas and examine current knowledge about the innovative technology of today – Blockchain.

Technical University, Varna

As part of our new partnership with Technical University of Varna, students can now attend new classes and learn how to code in one of the most powerful, scalable, and dynamic programming languages on any platform: Elixir.

New Bulgarian University, Sofia

Thanks to New Bulgarian University we have the opportunity to be part of a motivating and inspiring environment of students, with a desire for learning. As a recognizable specialist, NBU has invited Quanterall’s CEO, Anton Andonov, to be a lecturer in Erlang.

High School of Electronics, Varna

As a lecturer on Elixir in Professional Technical School, Varna, Assoc. Prof. Veselina Spasova, Ph.D. has proved that good teachers can build potential. A couple of students of PTG are now proud members of Quanterall’s teams which encouraged us to welcome new interns in the software and hardware entities.

Professional Technical School, Varna

Years after years of partnership, The High School of Electronics of Varna just keeps on surprising us!

Sofia Tech Park

We thank Sofia Tech Park for their trust and reliability! The most advanced high tech place in Bulgaria has granted us access to the Lab and project “Discoverer Petascale Supercomputer Bulgaria” – a consortium that combines the knowledge of over 15 years of experience in applied sciences.

Encourage & Grow

We recognize potential when we see it. That's why we invest our time and effort into turning students into highly qualified niche developers of quality software. Thanks to our endeavor, we are able to build teams with professionals with real, sustainable and flexible professional skills.
